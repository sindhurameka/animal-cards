Golden Retriever

Golden Retrievers instinctively love to swim. They're always up for a game of fetch in the lake.

  • Origin: Scotland
  • Weight: 55 - 75 lbs
  • Color: Shades of gold or cream
  • Average Lifespan: 11 years

Retrievers are well suited to residency in suburban or country environments. Although they need substantial outdoor exercise.Golden Retrievers also are very smart dogs that are loyal and friendly to their caregivers.
